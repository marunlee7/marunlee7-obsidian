## Description

IMPORTANT NOTE
This Pack doesn't contain the whole Obsidian Life OS Vault, you will still need to follow along with the series to get the best value out of this pack.

DOWNLOAD Contains
- Cycle and Reviews Templates
- MetaBind Cycles and Reviews Menu Buttons
- MetaBind Habit Tracker Widget
- Example of Month Logo with Month Directory

HOW TO USE PACK
1. Move templates into your templates folder and customise YAML to match your personal goals.
2. Add Daily, Weekly, Quarterly, Yearly Templates to your Periodic Notes Community Plugin
3. Customise your MetaBind Habit Tracker then change YAML to match in your Daily Journal Template
4. Create your Review Directories to match Metabind Cycles and Review Menu
5. Fill out your Action Plan 001
